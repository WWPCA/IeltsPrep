GDPR Compliance: This privacy policy complies with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and explains your rights regarding your personal data.
AI-Generated Content Disclosure: Our platform uses AI to generate educational content and assessments. We implement safety measures and user reporting features to ensure responsible AI use.

1. AI Technology and Content Safety

Our platform uses advanced AI technologies with built-in safety measures:

  • Content Filtering: AI-generated content is filtered for appropriateness
  • User Reporting: In-app reporting system for inappropriate content
  • Safety Monitoring: Continuous monitoring of AI outputs
  • Educational Purpose: AI content designed for educational IELTS preparation

2. Data Collection and Use

We collect and process personal data to provide our AI-powered IELTS assessment services:

  • Account Information: Email address, password (encrypted with bcrypt)
  • Assessment Data: Writing responses, speaking recordings, performance metrics
  • Usage Data: Assessment attempts, progress tracking, system logs
  • Safety Logs: AI interaction logs for safety monitoring

3. Your Rights (GDPR Articles 15-22)

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Rectification: Correct inaccurate data
  • Erasure: Delete your account and data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Portability: Export your data in a machine-readable format
  • Report Content: Use in-app reporting for inappropriate AI content
