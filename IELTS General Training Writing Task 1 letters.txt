IELTS General Training Writing Task 1 tests (letters) - 

1. 
Question:

Part 1

You should spend about 20 minutes on this task. Write at least 150 words.

You are currently enrolled in an evening course at a local community center, but you are facing several issues with the classroom environment that make it challenging to focus and learn effectively.

Write a letter to the course coordinator at the community center. In your letter:

describe the situation
explain your problems and why it is difficult to learn
suggest what kind of classroom environment you would prefer
You do NOT need to write any addresses.

Begin your letter as follows:

Dear Sir or Madam,
